# PaperKraken Privacy Policy

**PaperKraken collects no data. Period.**

- The plugin runs entirely on your machine inside your Claude Code session. It has no server, no telemetry, no analytics, and no account.
- Your papers and the generated reports never leave your computer through this plugin. All rendering (fonts, MathJax, Paged.js) is bundled locally and works offline.
- The only network requests involved are the ones **your own Claude session** makes at your request: downloading a paper from a URL you provide (e.g. arXiv) and fetching public abstract pages during the background-verification step. PaperKraken itself transmits nothing.
- One-time dependency installation (`npm`, `npx playwright install`, `uv`) contacts the respective public package registries, exactly as any manual install would.
